The beta launch of Klide is an early access phase where selected businesses can use our cloud POS solution before its official production release. This phase allows us to gather feedback, identify any issues, and make improvements based on real-world usage.

To participate in the beta launch, you can sign up through our website or contact our team directly. We are looking for a diverse range of businesses to test Klide and provide valuable feedback.

During the beta phase, you will have access to core features of Klide, including live billing, e-receipts, and digital billing. You’ll be able to experience how Klide streamlines operations and reduces paper waste.

Participation in the beta launch is free of charge. We value your feedback and in return, you get early access to our cutting-edge technology.

We offer dedicated support to all beta participants, including setup assistance, troubleshooting, and regular updates. Our team is here to ensure you have a smooth experience and to address any questions or concerns.

After the beta phase, we will review all feedback and make necessary improvements. Beta participants will be among the first to experience the finalized version of Klide, and we will provide details on how to transition smoothly to the production release.
