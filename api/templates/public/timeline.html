Early 2024

Conceptualization of Klide

In early 2024, Shailesh and Joel conceived a transformative approach to revolutionize billing systems by leveraging digital solutions. Their vision aimed to significantly reduce paper waste and the reliance on harmful chemicals, while ensuring affordability and accessibility for all businesses. This initiative prioritizes environmental sustainability and inclusivity, making cutting-edge payment solutions available to businesses of any size. Their innovative approach is set to redefine the future of point-of-sale systems worldwide.

  • Conducted initial market research to understand the needs of various businesses and pinpoint inefficiencies in traditional POS systems.
  • Built a prototype of Klide, focusing on user-friendly interfaces and streamlined functionalities for easy adoption by users.
